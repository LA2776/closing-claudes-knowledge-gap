We're back with some exciting new features to help you build different 💪
Imagine testing your projects on a real device with our brand new Local Run feature, or sprinkling some AI magic into your projects with Gemini AI Actions. And, because we love making things as intuitive as possible, we've got something special for onboarding your users – our new Walkthrough builder. Explore all the wonderful new features below ✨
Test on Device with Local Run 📲
We're thrilled to announce the launch of FlutterFlow Local Run, an exciting feature now available for MacOS users! With Local Run, you can now seamlessly test your application across multiple platforms all from within the FlutterFlow app. Key features include:
⚡ Instant Reloads: Experience the magic of seeing your app changes reflected instantly with the click of a button
⏳ Unlimited Test Duration: Dive deep into testing without watching the clock. With no time restrictions, you're free to explore every aspect of your app's performance and user experience in detail.
🤳 Native Device Testing: Test your app where it matters most — on actual devices. Ensure your app's look, feel, and functionality are exactly as you envision across IOS, Android, MacOS and Web!
New Gemini AI Actions 🪄
Explore the power of Google's Gemini AI directly within FlutterFlow with the new Gemini actions! Generate text, accept text-and-image inputs, and count tokens easily by simply adding your Gemini API key in settings and adding action within the action flow editor. Add unbelievable AI features to your FlutterFlow app in just a few clicks!
Seamless App Walkthroughs 🔎
This exciting new feature allows you to create engaging, custom-designed walkthroughs for your app directly within FlutterFlow! With customizable shapes, colors, and use of components, these walkthroughs can easily be customized and triggered via an action in the action flow editor. Watch the short tutorial video below and add a personalized touch to your FlutterFlow app.
Flutter 3.19 Upgrade 📥
We officially upgraded FlutterFlow to Flutter 3.19! The new Flutter version includes two new SDK releases, general bug fixes and Gemini integrations. Make sure to review your custom code for any outdated dependencies. While most pubspec dependencies are compatible, updates may be required for some older packages.
Other Improvements 🛠️
More support for enums (e.g. enums in app state, enums in drop downs)
Query Supabase action
Usability fixes (e.g. more options for replacing widgets)
Code generation improvements
