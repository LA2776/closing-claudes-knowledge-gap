Hey everyone,
We wanted to follow up on the pricing update announcement before we roll into the weekend on the West coast- thank you for all of your passionate feedback. We’re sorry that our first pass missed the mark. We’ve read every breakdown, and proposal. Some of them were hard to hear, but every comment helped us see the gaps.
Here are some immediate changes (full details to follow by Jun 11th).
Growth plan → Introducing a Single-Seat Option, Adding 2 Feature Branches
Starts at one seat and now includes branching (main + 2 feature branches). Among other features, this tier includes access to GitHub integration, the VS Code extension, and limited access to snapshots. 
The full price for the two-person Growth plan will remain roughly $135, but a cheaper option will be available for solo developers. 
Business plan → Introducing a Single-Seat Option
Similar to the Growth plan, the Business plan will be available as a one-seat plan at a reduced cost, with the ability to add up to 4 more seats on a pay-per-seat basis.
“Collaborator Pass” Add-On
A single-project, single-seat add-on so freelancers can jump into a client project (or invite a client in) without forcing a big plan upgrade. 
2 API calls per project will be available for the free plan.
Access to some recent snapshots will be available in every tier, but will expand at higher tiers. More details to come.
And clarifications based on comments we’ve seen: 
Existing Annual Subscribers: Receive a credit when selecting a new plan or request a prorated refund for the unused portion of your term. No hoops, no fees.
Regional discounts still apply at the same percentages as before.
We’ll share a full feature matrix by June 11th.
Thank you for holding us to a high standard. FlutterFlow exists because of this community and your willingness to speak up is part of what makes it strong. 🙏
We appreciate all of you.
Abel & Alex
