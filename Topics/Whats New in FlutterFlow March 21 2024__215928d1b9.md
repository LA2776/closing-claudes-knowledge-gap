We're starting this update with some big news - FlutterFlow for Windows is here! Dive into a world where performance meets convenience, all tailored for your Windows machine. And that's just the beginning.
Imagine executing actions in your app simultaneously with our new Parallel Actions, or crafting complex queries with ease thanks to Firestore OR Queries. Plus, with API Interceptors, gain unparalleled control over your app's interactions. Keep reading to explore all of the exciting new features!
FlutterFlow for Windows ✨
We're absolutely thrilled to share that FlutterFlow for Windows has officially launched! This eagerly awaited release brings the power of FlutterFlow to your Windows environment, optimizing app responsiveness and leveraging the robust performance and speed of your machine. It's all about making your app development smoother and more efficient, right from the comfort of your Windows device.
👉 Download FlutterFlow for Windows
New Parallel Actions 🔀
Parallel Actions now enable simultaneous execution of actions within your app. Asynchronous actions can be performed at the same time, optimizing performance and streamlining user interactions! This feature is designed to make apps more efficient, reflecting our ongoing efforts to enhance development capabilities.
Firestore OR Queries 🤷‍♂️
We've introduced the ability to use AND/OR queries within your Firestore queries, significantly improving the flexibility and power of your database interactions. This update allows for more complex and dynamic data retrieval, making your app development process even smoother!
API Interceptors 🛜
We're excited to announce the availability of API Interceptors! This feature allows you to intercept and modify API requests and responses, providing enhanced control over your app's external interactions. By enabling custom headers, request body manipulation, and response processing, API Interceptors empower you to tailor API communications to your specific needs, ensuring more secure and efficient app functionality.
Download Code Shortcut via CLI 📥
We've made it even easier to export your project directly to your local device with the new FlutterFlow CLI snippet. Now, there's no need to copy and paste your project ID manually; simply click the icon, copy the provided snippet, and paste it into your terminal. It's these small yet impactful improvements that we believe significantly enhance the developer experience.
