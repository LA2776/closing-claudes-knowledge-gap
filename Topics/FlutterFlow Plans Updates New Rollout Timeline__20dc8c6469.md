Hey everyone,
As promised, we’re back with the full feature matrix and updated pricing details.
The last week has been intense. When we announced the new plans with you last week, we heard loud and clear that it needed changes. We appreciate all of the thoughtfulness and passion in sharing your feedback and this latest update reflects that.
The Full Matrix is Live
We’ve published the full breakdown of each plan. You’ll see where features like branching, GitHub, snapshots and collaboration features land across the tiers.
View the full feature matrix: https://docs.flutterflow.io/accounts-billing/plan-comparison/
Want to revisit how we got here? You can see the conversation here.
Updated Rollout Timeline
To give everyone time to navigate the changes:
We’re pushing the official rollout back to August 18, 2025.
You’ll have a 30-day window from that date to choose the plan that best fits your needs.
If you don’t select a new plan during that window, you’ll be automatically moved to the one that most closely matches your current usage.
For annual subscribers: You can request a prorated refund or apply a credit toward your new plan.
This past week has reminded us about how deeply you care about FlutterFlow and we’re grateful you’re here. 🙏
Abel & Alex
