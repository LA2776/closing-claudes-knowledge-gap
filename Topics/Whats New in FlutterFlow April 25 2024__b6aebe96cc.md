Dive into some exciting new features including notification count badges for iOS, support for Apple's privacy manifest, a redesigned color picker and so much more. Let’s explore what’s new!
Adaptive Icon Support 📱
FlutterFlow now supports Adaptive Icons for Android, ensuring your app icons look sleek and professional across all devices and launcher themes. This new feature allows you to specify app icon backgrounds which automatically adjust with the app icon to match different user interface environments.
Notification Count Badge Actions 🔔
This exciting new action enables your app to display notification count badges, providing users with a visual prompt to engage with your app's updates or new content. It's a simple yet powerful way to enhance user interaction and keep your audience informed and connected.
Redesigned Color Picker 🎨
The color picker has been redesigned with your feedback in mind! We've added a convenient 'clear color' icon directly on the color selector, saving you an extra click and streamlining your design process. With its improved UI, selecting and managing colors in your projects is now more intuitive and efficient than ever.
Apple Privacy Manifest Support 🔒
FlutterFlow now supports the Apple privacy manifest, helping you manage privacy permissions more effectively within your iOS apps. This update ensures that your applications adhere to the latest privacy requirements by Apple, offering a straightforward way to handle user data permissions and enhance trust. Note that the privacy manifest is required for Apple approval starting May 1.
Safe Area Property for Containers 🚧
We've enhanced the flexibility of layout design in FlutterFlow with the new Safe Area property for containers. Now, you can ensure critical content, like navigation elements or crucial text, doesn't get obscured by device-specific features such as notches or rounded corners. This property is particularly useful when you want to combine full-page backgrounds that extend into the edges with overlay content that requires visibility and accessibility assurance.
