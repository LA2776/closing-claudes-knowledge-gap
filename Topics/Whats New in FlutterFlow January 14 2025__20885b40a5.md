Libraries as Design Systems
Elevate your design workflow with Libraries as Design Systems. Now, you can create and maintain consistent visual elements across all your projects in FlutterFlow, streamlining your design process and ensuring uniformity and brand consistency with ease. Simply select a Library as a Design System in the Design Settings page.
You can read more in the documentation.
Firestore Support for Libraries
We're excited to announce expanded Firestore support for Libraries in FlutterFlow. Now, you can effortlessly import and reuse Firestore collections from libraries across different projects. This update simplifies data management and enhances project scalability by allowing you to maintain consistent data structures and streamline workflows across your development team. 
See the documentation for more details.
