From customizable canvas colors to sophisticated animation controls and the flexibility to update branch names post-creation, these new features are designed to make your development process smoother and more intuitive. Dive into these new capabilities and see how they can elevate your projects!
Custom Canvas Color 🎨
Improve your development process with the new canvas color option in FlutterFlow! This feature allows you to set a canvas color within the builder, making it easier to view components that lack a specified background color. Tailor your builder environment to your preferences and improve visibility, ensuring every element of your component stands out during the development process.
Animation Values by Variable 🔢
Take your app's animations to the next level with FlutterFlow's new feature allowing you to set animation values by variable. Now you can create sophisticated delayed effects, such as staggering list animations based on index, adding a visually engaging element to your app. Customize timing and sequence with precision, and watch your app come alive with smooth, dynamic animations that capture user attention.
Update Branch Names After Creation ✍️
FlutterFlow now allows you to update branch names even after they've been created, providing enhanced flexibility in managing your project's version control. This feature is perfect for keeping your development workflow organized and adaptive to changes, ensuring that your branch names always reflect their current purpose or stage of development.
