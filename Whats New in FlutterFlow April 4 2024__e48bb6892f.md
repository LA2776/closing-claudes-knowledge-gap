FlutterFlow is back! We hope you had a good laugh this April Fools and got creative with your custom designs using the FabricFlow Editor.
It's time to dive into some exciting features, including the new Drag & Drop functionality, personalized IDE configurations and UI Builder Value fields. These updates are all about making your FlutterFlow experience even more intuitive and tailored just for you.
Drag & Drop Widget 🤏
Get ready to take your app's user experience to the next level with our newest feature: Drag & Drop Widgets. This innovative addition to FlutterFlow allows you to enhance interactivity by enabling users to drag widgets across the screen and drop them onto designated targets. Customize the experience further by selecting data to transfer between widgets and defining actions for hover enter, exit, and drop acceptance. This feature not only boosts app functionality but also opens up a world of creative possibilities for user engagement and interactivity!
Google Sign In for Windows 🔐
Following your feedback, we're thrilled to bring Google Sign-In to the FlutterFlow Windows app. Now, logging into FlutterFlow on your Windows device is as smooth and secure as ever, thanks to this much-requested feature. Haven't tried FlutterFlow for Windows? Click below to download!
Download FlutterFlow for Windows 🔗
Local Run IDE Configuration 💾
Tailor your FlutterFlow experience with the new Local Run IDE Configuration options, where you can now choose your preferred IDE, such as VS Code, Android Studio, or IntelliJ. This update provides a personalized development environment, ensuring that your workflow is more efficient and aligned with your favorite tools. Set up your IDE with just a few clicks and enjoy a custom-tailored coding experience designed for your comfort and productivity.
UI Builder Value 🔠
Enhance your UI building experience with our new UI Builder Display Value field, allowing you to rename variable names for clarity and efficiency within the builder. This neat feature gives you the power to organize your workspace just the way you like, making it easier than ever to navigate and manage your project elements.
